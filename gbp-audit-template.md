## Part 5 — 10 GBP Posts

### 5.1 Post Drafting Guidelines

Every GBP post must include:

1. **Target Keyword** — naturally integrated, not stuffed.
2. **Local Anchor** — a reference to the service area, neighborhood, or city that grounds the post geographically.
3. **Emotional Hook** — a line that speaks to a pain point, aspiration, or urgency the reader feels.
4. **CTA** — a clear call to action (call, book, visit website, get directions).

Posts should be 150-300 words. Write in the client's brand voice. Each post should be copy-paste ready.

## SEO Health Score

### Scoring Methodology

Rate each section based on the audit findings. The total possible score is 100 points.

| Section | Max Points | Weight Rationale | Score |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Schema Markup** | 30 | Structured data is the technical foundation for rich results and local pack eligibility. Errors here directly harm visibility. | /30 |
| **Keywords** | 20 | Targeting the right queries determines whether traffic has conversion potential. | /20 |
| **Content** | 20 | Content depth and gap coverage build topical authority and support keyword targeting. | /20 |
| **GBP Completeness** | 15 | A fully optimized profile improves local pack ranking and conversion from search. | /15 |
| **Reviews** | 15 | Review volume, rating, recency, and response rate are direct local ranking and trust signals. | /15 |
| **TOTAL** | **100** | | **/100** |

### Scoring Rubric

**Schema Markup (30 points)**
- 25-30: Valid JSON-LD with all core properties, additional schema types (FAQ, Person, WebSite), no errors.
- 15-24: JSON-LD present with most core properties, minor gaps, no critical errors.
- 5-14: JSON-LD present but incomplete, some errors, missing important properties.
- 0-4: No JSON-LD, critical errors, or fundamentally broken implementation.

**Keywords (20 points)**
- 16-20: 20 well-researched keywords with clear intent mapping, good mix of types, all mapped to content.
- 10-15: Solid keyword list but some gaps in coverage or intent mapping.
- 5-9: Limited research, missing key categories, weak intent alignment.
- 0-4: No keyword strategy or severely misaligned targeting.

**Content (20 points)**
- 16-20: Few or no content gaps vs. competitors, strong service pages, active blog or resource section.
- 10-15: Some gaps identified but core services well covered.
- 5-9: Significant gaps, thin service pages, minimal supporting content.
- 0-4: Major content deficiencies across the board.

**GBP Completeness (15 points)**
- 12-15: All profile fields complete, photos competitive, Q&A active, categories optimized.
- 8-11: Most fields complete, some photo or Q&A gaps.
- 4-7: Incomplete profile, outdated photos, missing key fields.
- 0-3: Bare-minimum profile with major gaps.

**Reviews (15 points)**
- 12-15: Strong review volume and rating, high response rate, active generation strategy.
- 8-11: Decent volume and rating, some responses, room for improvement.
- 4-7: Low volume or rating issues, inconsistent responses.
- 0-3: Very few reviews, no responses, or significant reputation issues.

### Score Interpretation

| Range | Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 85-100 | Excellent | Minor refinements only. Focus on maintaining and scaling. |
| 70-84 | Good | Solid foundation with clear opportunities for improvement. |
| 50-69 | Fair | Significant gaps that are likely costing visibility and leads. |
| 30-49 | Poor | Major overhaul needed across multiple areas. |
| 0-29 | Critical | Fundamental issues requiring immediate attention. |
