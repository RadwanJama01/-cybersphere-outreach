## Usage Notes

- All image prompts are written in natural-language style compatible with DALL-E, Midjourney, and Nano Banana Pro (Gemini image generation).
- For Midjourney, append `--ar 1:1` or `--ar 4:5` as indicated. Use `--v 6` or later for best results.
- For DALL-E, specify the aspect ratio in the generation settings rather than the prompt.
- For Nano Banana Pro, prompts work as-is with no modifications.
- Phone number +1 (325) 240-6238 appears in every ad's CTA and should be included in any text overlays added during final creative production.
- Image prompts do NOT include text overlays for headlines/CTAs — add those in your design tool (Canva, Figma, etc.) after generation.
