## Step 1 — Create the Notion Workspace

**Service:** Notion — https://www.notion.so
**What you're building:** A workspace with 7 interconnected databases that form the content engine's backbone.

### 1.1 Create the workspace root page

1. Navigate to https://www.notion.so
2. In the left sidebar, click "+ New page" (or press Ctrl+N / Cmd+N)
3. A blank page opens. In the title field at the top, type "Cybersphere Content Engine"
4. Press Enter to confirm the title
5. The page is now your workspace root. All databases will be created inside this page.
